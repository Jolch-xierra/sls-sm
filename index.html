Page 3 – Meaning & Jobs/Skills

Page 3 – Why Do People Work in Sport?

People in sport help others grow, feel confident, and work as a team. A good coach demonstrates care, respect, and perseverance daily in their training sessions.

Short Story: Coach A

Coach A trains the school soccer team. When players make mistakes, Coach A doesn't get angry. Instead, Coach A stops play, explains with care, and encourages them to try again. When a player got injured during a match, Coach A immediately rushed to check on them and guided them safely to the first aid station.

Pre-University – Designing My Sports-Related Pathway

Connecting personal values, strengths, structural sports industry clusters, and post-secondary pathways in Singapore.
Page 1 – Introduction & Hook

Page 1 – Designing My Sports-Related Pathway

Pre-University is a critical junction. Navigating the athletic landscape at an advanced level means mapping professional accreditation boards, industry pillars, and advanced tertiary programs.

Pre-U Learning Goals Checklist:

The Five Core Sports Clusters in Singapore:

1. High-Performance & Sports Science

Biomechanics, physiology, video analysis, coaching pedagogy.

2. Sports Medicine & Rehabilitation

Physiotherapy, clinical orthopaedics, occupational therapy.

3. Sports Business, Management & Events

Sponsorship sales, facilities operations, club admin.

4. Sports Media, Communications & Technology

Journalism, broadcasting, wearable tech development.

5. Education, Coaching & Community Development

ActiveSG managers, PE teachers, fitness instructors.
